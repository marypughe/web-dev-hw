Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic,,crushed
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Meltbutter in a medium saucepan over medium lowheat
  2. Addcream and simmerfor 5 minutes
  3. Addgarlic and cheese and whiskquickly, heating through
  4. stirin parsley and serve
