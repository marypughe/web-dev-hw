Rhubarb Pie Filling

This is a great tasting, and easy to make pie filling. All you need is to buy a crust, or add some crumble and you have yourself a dessert.

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups rhubarb sliced into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstartch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsps lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp salted butter

DIRECTIONS

\
Add All of the ingredients into a medium size pot
Cook over medium hest for 5- 10 minutes
Stir often so it doess not burn
Simmer for another 5-10 minutes
Done when rhubarb is soft
